Recipe of the month

Chiken Biryani

To make chicken biryani, start by rinsing and soaking 2 cups of basmati rice in cold water for 30 minutes. In a large pot or pressure cooker, sauté 2 thinly sliced onions in 2 tablespoons of oil and 2 tablespoons of ghee until they turn golden brown. Then, add 2 tablespoons of ginger garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes. Next, add 1 kg of boneless chicken cut into small pieces and cook until it's no longer pink. Add 3 chopped tomatoes, 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of cumin powder, 1 teaspoon of coriander powder, 1 teaspoon of garam masala powder, and 1 teaspoon of red chili powder. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes. Add 1 cup of yogurt and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked. In a separate pot, boil water and add the rice. Cook until the rice is half-done, drain the water, and set the rice aside. In the same pot, add 2 tablespoons of oil and 2 tablespoons of ghee. Add 2 bay leaves, 2 cinnamon sticks, 4 cardamom pods, and 4 cloves. Sauté for 2 minutes. Add a layer of half-cooked rice and then a layer of chicken curry. Repeat the layers until the rice and curry are used up. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita and papad.
